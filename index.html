JEE

The benefits of taking up JEE Main can be plenty. Firstly, clearing the JEE Main Exam will help a candidate qualify to write the exam of JEE Advanced. If a student cracks the competition on both levels, he/she can get entry into the top-most colleges across the country for any major course such as B.Tech, B.Arch, and B.Plan, and many more.

NEET

NEET or the National Eligibility cum Entrance Test is a medical entrance test for aspirants seeking admittance into medical institutions to study courses such as MBBS, BDS, BHMS, BUMS etc in both government and private medical institutes across the country

CET
